About us

Welcome to Reyvaa International, a striving player in industrial supply of Dried herbs, Dehydrated or spray Dried vegetables & fruits, Spices and condiments , whether dried or fresh. Whether you're a food manufacturer, restaurant, or catering business, we have the experience and resources to help you find the right herbs and spices for your needs.
